The Leech Database

The Leech Database (Ldb) is an online resource dedicated to providing experimentally verified information about proteins found in various species of leech.

Sources: UniProtKB === Developed by: Department of Computational Biology, Institute of Bioinformatics, Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences
